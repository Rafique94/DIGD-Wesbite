About Us

Daga Investment and General Dealers is a Malawian company that was established in 2010 as a contractor in general trading under the Business Names Registration Act Cap 46:02 in Malawi. Our certificate number is 130836. Our founder and current Managing Director, Douglas Nang'ombe, is a well-respected figure in the IT, construction, and agriculture industries. We are headquartered in Blantyre, with a subsidiary in Mchinji. Our mission is to expand further throughout Malawi and explore new markets, while creating employment opportunities for Malawians and reducing poverty and unemployment levels in the country. We are committed to working with the Malawi government to achieve the Malawi Growth and Development Strategy (MGDS) by providing long-lasting services and creating sustainable jobs.

Our Vision

Our vision at Daga Investment and General Dealers is to be recognized as the leading provider of computers equipment, accessories and repairs, offering exceptional value to our customers through affordable pricing and superior customer service. We are committed to putting our customers first in all that we do, and aim to exceed their expectations by continuously improving our products, services, and processes.

Our Mission

At Daga Investment and General Dealers, our mission is to provide exceptional services that not only meet, but exceed our customers' needs. We strive for excellence in all that we do, and hold ourselves accountable for delivering high-quality work with professionalism and respect. Our values of accountability, flexibility, and professionalism guide us in our commitment to providing the best services in the country.
